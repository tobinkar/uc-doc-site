# Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

---

## Getting Started

### How do I create my first course?
See our [Create Your First Course](../getting-started/first-course.md) guide for step-by-step instructions.

### Can I import content from another platform?
Currently, you need to upload content manually. We're working on import tools for common LMS platforms.

### How many courses can I create?
Essential plan: Up to 100 courses. Pro and above: Unlimited courses.

---

## Courses & Content

### What video formats are supported?
MP4, MOV, and WebM. Maximum file size is 5GB per video.

### Can learners download videos?
By default, no. You can enable downloads per course in Course Settings → Content → Allow Downloads.

### How long does video processing take?
Usually 1-2 minutes for standard videos. Longer videos (1+ hour) may take up to 10 minutes.

### Can I embed YouTube videos instead of uploading?
Yes! Use the Embed lesson type and paste your YouTube URL.

### Do you support SCORM?
Not currently. SCORM import is on our roadmap for 2026.

## Learners & Access

### Is there a limit to how many learners I can have?
No. All plans include unlimited learners.

### Can learners access courses on mobile?
Yes. Unicourse works in mobile browsers. Native iOS/Android apps coming soon.

### How do learners reset their password?
They click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

### Can I organize learners into groups?
Yes. Go to Learners → Groups to create teams, departments, or cohorts.

---

## Quizzes & Certificates

### Can I require a passing score?
Yes. In quiz settings, set the passing score percentage. Learners must pass to continue or receive a certificate.

### How do I issue certificates?
Enable certificates in Course Settings. Learners receive them automatically upon completion (if they meet requirements).

### Can I customize the certificate design?
Yes. Go to Settings → Certificates to upload your own template or customize colors and text.

### Can learners retake quizzes?
You control this per quiz. Set "Attempts Allowed" in quiz settings (1 = no retakes, unlimited = infinite retakes).

## Branding & Customization

### Can I use my own domain?
Yes! Set up a custom domain in Settings → Branding. See [Custom Branding](../branding/custom-branding.md) for instructions.

### How do I remove the "Powered by Unicourse" badge?
This requires a Pro or Business plan with white-label enabled. Contact support to enable.

### Can I customize the login page?
You can add your logo and colors. Full login page customization is available on Enterprise plans.

## Billing & Account

### How do I upgrade my plan?
Go to Settings → Billing → Change Plan. See [Billing](../account/billing.md) for details.

### Do you offer refunds?
Yes. We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions.

### Can I pay annually?
Yes. Annual billing saves you 2 months (pay for 10, get 12).

### What happens if I cancel?
You retain access until the end of your billing period. Data is kept for 30 days after, then deleted.

## Technical Issues

### Video won't play
Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page
2. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
3. Clear browser cache
4. Check your internet connection

If problems persist, contact support with the video URL.

### I can't upload files
Check:
- File size (max 5GB for video, 100MB for documents)
- File format (see supported types)
- Browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)

### Course progress isn't saving
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check for browser extensions blocking storage
- Try disabling ad blockers

### Emails aren't being received
Check spam/junk folder. Add `@unicourse.io` to your safe senders. If using custom domain, verify SPF/DKIM records.
