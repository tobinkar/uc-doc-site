# Set Up Your Account

After signing up, follow these steps to configure your organization.

## Step 1: Complete Your Profile

1. Go to **Settings → Profile**
2. Add your name and profile photo
3. Set your timezone (affects scheduled notifications)
4. Click **Save Changes**

## Step 2: Configure Your Organization

1. Go to **Settings → Organization**
2. Enter your organization name
3. Upload your company logo (recommended: 200x200px PNG)
4. Set your primary brand color
5. Click **Save**

[Screenshot: Organization settings page]

## Step 3: Set Up Your Domain (Optional)

Use your own domain for a fully branded experience (e.g., `training.yourcompany.com`).

1. Go to **Settings → Branding → Custom Domain**
2. Enter your desired subdomain
3. Add the CNAME record to your DNS provider:
   ```
   Type: CNAME
   Name: training (or your choice)
   Value: custom.unicourse.io
   ```
4. Click **Verify Domain**
5. Allow up to 24 hours for DNS propagation

## Step 4: Invite Team Members

Add admins or instructors who will help manage courses.

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role:
   - **Admin** — Full access to all settings
   - **Instructor** — Create/edit courses, view analytics
   - **Support** — View learners and progress (read-only)
5. Click **Send Invite**

Your account is ready. Time to create your first course.
