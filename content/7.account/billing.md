# Billing & Subscriptions

Manage your plan, payment methods, and invoices.

## Plans Overview

| Plan | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Essential | $29/mo | 100+ courses, basic analytics, quizzes |
| Pro | $79/mo | Unlimited courses, advanced analytics, certificates, priority support |
| Business | $199/mo | Everything in Pro + SSO, API access, custom integrations |
| Enterprise | Custom | Volume pricing, dedicated support, SLA |

All plans include:
- Unlimited learners
- Video hosting
- Mobile access
- Email support

## Viewing Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. See your current plan and usage
3. View next billing date

## Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Review prorated charges
5. Click **Confirm Upgrade**

Upgrades take effect immediately. You're charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle.

## Downgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select the lower plan
4. Review what you'll lose access to
5. Click **Confirm Downgrade**

Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You retain access to all features until then.

## Payment Methods

### Add a Payment Method
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Enter card details
4. Click **Save**

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.

### Update Payment Method
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**
2. Click **Edit** on your current method
3. Update details
4. Click **Save**

### Remove Payment Method
You must have at least one active payment method on file.

## Invoices

### View Invoices
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Invoices**
2. See all past invoices
3. Click any invoice to view details

### Download Invoice
1. Click on an invoice
2. Click **Download PDF**

### Invoice Emails
Invoices are automatically emailed to the billing contact. To change:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Billing Contact**
2. Update the email address
3. Click **Save**

## Annual Billing

Save with annual payment (2 months free).

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Switch to Annual**
3. Review the annual price
4. Click **Confirm**

To switch back to monthly, contact support@unicourse.io.

## Cancellation

### Cancel Subscription
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Tell us why (optional)
4. Click **Confirm Cancellation**

After cancellation:
- Access continues until end of billing period
- Learners retain access until end of billing period
- Data is retained for 30 days, then deleted
- You can reactivate anytime within 30 days

### Reactivate
If you cancelled recently:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Reactivate Subscription**
3. Confirm your payment method
4. Click **Reactivate**

## Refunds

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions.

To request a refund:
1. Email support@unicourse.io
2. Include your account email
3. Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

## Tax Information

### Tax Exemption
If your organization is tax-exempt:
1. Email support@unicourse.io
2. Attach your exemption certificate
3. We'll update your account within 2 business days

### VAT (EU Customers)
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Tax Information**
2. Enter your VAT ID
3. Click **Validate**

Valid VAT IDs apply reverse-charge (no VAT on invoice).
