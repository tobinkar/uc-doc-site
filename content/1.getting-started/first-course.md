# Create Your First Course

Get your first course live in about 10 minutes.

## Step 1: Create a New Course

1. From the Dashboard, click **+ New Course**
2. Enter a course title (e.g., "Employee Onboarding")
3. Add a description (2-3 sentences explaining what learners will gain)
4. Upload a cover image (recommended: 1280x720px)
5. Click **Create Course**

[Screenshot: New course modal]

## Step 2: Add Your Content

Courses are organized into **Modules**, and modules contain **Lessons**.

### Create a Module
1. Click **+ Add Module**
2. Enter a module title (e.g., "Getting Started")
3. Click **Save**

### Add a Lesson
1. Inside your module, click **+ Add Lesson**
2. Choose a lesson type:
   - **Video** — Upload or embed video content
   - **Document** — PDFs, slides, or text
   - **Quiz** — Test learner knowledge
   - **Assignment** — Open-ended submissions
3. Enter the lesson title
4. Add your content
5. Click **Save Lesson**

### Upload a Video
1. Click **Upload Video** or drag and drop your file
2. Supported formats: MP4, MOV, WebM (max 5GB)
3. Wait for processing (usually 1-2 minutes)
4. Add optional video description or transcript

[Screenshot: Video upload interface]

## Step 3: Configure Course Settings

1. Click the **Settings** tab in your course
2. Set access type:
   - **Open** — Anyone with the link can enroll
   - **Invite Only** — Only invited learners can access
   - **Paid** — Require payment to enroll (coming soon)
3. Enable/disable certificates
4. Set completion requirements (e.g., complete all lessons, pass quiz)
5. Click **Save Settings**

## Step 4: Publish Your Course

1. Preview your course by clicking **Preview**
2. When ready, click **Publish**
3. Your course is now live and can accept learners

## Tips for Great Courses

- **Keep videos short** — 5-10 minutes per video works best
- **Add quizzes** — Reinforce learning with knowledge checks
- **Use clear titles** — Help learners navigate easily
- **Include resources** — Downloadable docs add value
