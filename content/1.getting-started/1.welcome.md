# Welcome to Unicourse

Unicourse is an enterprise learning platform that helps you create, manage, and deliver professional training courses to your teams, customers, and learners.

## What You Can Do

**Create Professional Courses**
Build engaging courses with videos, documents, quizzes, and assessments. No technical expertise required.

**Manage Your Learners**
Invite team members, track progress, and manage access all from one dashboard.

**Customize Your Brand**
Add your logo, colors, and custom domain for a seamless branded experience.

**Measure Success**
Get detailed analytics on course completion, learner progress, and content effectiveness.

## Platform Overview

### Dashboard
Your home base. See recent activity, course stats, and quick actions.

### Courses
Create and manage your course catalog. Each course can contain multiple modules with videos, documents, and quizzes.

### Learners
View all enrolled users, track their progress, and manage access.

### Analytics
Detailed reports on completion rates, engagement, and learner performance.

### Settings
Configure your organization, branding, team members, and billing.
