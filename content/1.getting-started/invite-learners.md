# Invite Learners

Add learners to your courses individually or in bulk.

## Option 1: Email Invitation

Best for small groups or individual invites.

1. Go to your course → **Learners** tab
2. Click **Invite Learners**
3. Enter email addresses (one per line or comma-separated)
4. Optionally add a personal message
5. Click **Send Invitations**

Learners receive an email with a link to create their account and enroll.

[Screenshot: Invite modal]

## Option 2: Shareable Link

Best for open enrollment or embedding on your website.

1. Go to your course → **Settings** → **Enrollment**
2. Copy the **Enrollment Link**
3. Share via email, your website, or internal tools
4. Anyone with the link can self-enroll

**Pro tip:** Enable "Require approval" if you want to review enrollments before granting access.

## Option 3: Bulk Import (CSV)

Best for adding many learners at once.

1. Go to **Learners** (main navigation)
2. Click **Import Learners**
3. Download the CSV template
4. Fill in learner details:
   ```csv
   email,first_name,last_name,course_id
   john@example.com,John,Smith,course_abc123
   jane@example.com,Jane,Doe,course_abc123
   ```
5. Upload your completed CSV
6. Review the import preview
7. Click **Import**

Learners receive welcome emails with login instructions.

## Learner Experience

After receiving an invitation, learners:
1. Click the link in their email
2. Create a password (or sign in if they have an account)
3. Land on the course dashboard
4. Begin learning

## Managing Enrollments

### View Enrolled Learners
Go to **Course → Learners** to see everyone enrolled in a course.

### Remove a Learner
1. Find the learner in the list
2. Click the **⋮** menu → **Remove from Course**
3. Confirm removal

Their progress is retained in case you re-enroll them later.

### Resend Invitation
If a learner didn't receive their invite:
1. Find them in **Course → Learners**
2. Click **⋮** menu → **Resend Invitation**
